Cohort Server

The Cohort Server acts as the guts of the operation, allowing you to control and talk to your devices. This acts as the middle-man between your cueing source (QLab, Isadora, or our admin website) and smartphone(s). We host a server in the cloud for general use, development, and workshopping.
